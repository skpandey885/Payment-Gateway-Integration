We at Sparks Donation helps the needy people to survive on this planet. We believe that poverty is not permanent, and that people are not defined by their current circumstances. Our mission is to serve those in poverty by providing crisis care, case management, transformative education, food and household subsidy, employment training and active referrals to other collaborative social service providers.

Our Purpose

To inspire and empower people living in poverty to lift themselves and their families to economic self-sufficiency.
